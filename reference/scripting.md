# Writing the video script

Turn cleaned page text into a spoken narration script.

## Structure

1. **Hook** (1 sentence) — why this matters.
2. **Body** (2–4 points) — the key takeaways, in plain language.
3. **Close** (1 sentence) — the takeaway or call to action.

## Rules

- Write for the ear, not the eye: short sentences, active voice.
- ~150 words ≈ 1 minute of narration. Keep total under 90 seconds.
- Drop jargon unless the source audience clearly expects it.
- One idea per sentence so narration timing is predictable.

Use `assets/script_template.md` as the starting structure and fill each section.
